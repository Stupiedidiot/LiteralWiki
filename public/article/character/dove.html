Dove

Gender Female
Significant Other Chicken Man

Introduced in the Prologue of TWOLH, Dove is the significant other Chicken Man. She appears to be a friendly and quiet individual. Not much is known about her since she quickly became Hat's first victim.

Appearance

Dove has long hair and is typically seen wearing a tank top with a dove imprint on it.
